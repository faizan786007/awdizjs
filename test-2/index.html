Welcome to My Adventures

I base my adventures from my home in Seattle, Washington. Sometimes my adventures are just a bus ride away, but other times, they involve exhausting flights to far away international locations. This blog covers not only my adventures, but the conditioning and training that keeps me fit and ready. Thank you for being a part of the journey!

My Passion for Fitness

I wasn't always an active person. After some setbacks in my health, I joined up with a gym. That gym offered a number of outings. Once I saw what I could do with a stronger, fitter body, I was hooked. Since starting this journey, I have climbed mountains and kayaked down rivers. I have gone in caves and up canyons. My passion for fitness is based on living life to its fullest.
